Policies

Ethical Hacking

We practice cybersecurity only in authorized, educational environments such as online labs, CTF competitions, and guided workshops.

Testing school networks, bypassing security controls, or violating privacy is strictly prohibited.

Student Data

We collect minimal information—name, email, grade, and interests—solely for organizing activities and communication.

Data is stored securely in Google Workspace and accessed only by student organizers.

We do not share or sell personal information.

Media & Communications

Photos and recordings are shared only with consent.

Members may request removal of their images at any time.

Official updates are distributed through email and our communication platform.

Compliance & Status

This project is student-led and currently independent.

On-campus activities will follow school and district rules once official approval is granted.
