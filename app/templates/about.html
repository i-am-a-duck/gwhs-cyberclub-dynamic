Mission

Our mission is to inspire and empower students to master cybersecurity skills, build a strong foundation in ethical hacking and defense, and prepare the next generation of digital guardians.

We stand at the crossroads of technology and responsibility, cultivating both technical expertise and the integrity to use it wisely.

What We Do

  • Hands-on Workshops & CTFs — from beginner-friendly picoCTF to advanced challenges, guided labs, and competition prep.
  • Competition Training — CyberPatriot, national and international contests that test skill and teamwork under pressure.
  • Conferences & Knowledge Sharing — insights and takeaways from industry events like BSidesSF, RSA, and DEF CON.

What We Don’t

  • No unauthorized testing
  • No disruption of school networks
  • No violation of privacy or ethical standards
